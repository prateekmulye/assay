# Naming Brief — Final Council Decision

## 1. Assay (12 pts — unanimous first choice)
**Identity:** To assay is to test metal for purity and stamp a verified mark — the oldest verification metaphor in the language.

**Why chosen:** Ranked first by all four members. It names the value hypothesis (verification-as-product) rather than the architecture, sells provenance to time-poor RIA/agent-builder buyers in one syllable, and gives the product a working verb ("every claim assayed") for chip micro-copy, hover states, and verify actions. Skeptic-VC confirmed it survives a compliance officer's sniff test with zero fintech collisions.

**Honest risks:** Reads biotech/lab to life-sciences people. assay.ai registered since 2019 (redirects, possibly quietly in use). getassay.com and useassay.com were both registered in 2026 — someone is circling this brand. Cost-of-delay is real; verify who holds those domains immediately.

**Domain form:** `assay.dev` or `assayhq.com` primary; pursue acquisition of `assay.ai` in parallel.

## 2. Verdix (4 pts, wins tiebreak on two second-place votes)
**Identity:** Verdict + x — a coined proper noun named for the `verdicts` table, the graded ledger that is the product's only non-backfillable asset.

**Why chosen:** Product-owner and growth both ranked it second. Distinctive, trademarkable, pronounceable in every major language, and reads as a name rather than a description — avoiding the insider-jargon trap.

**Honest risks:** Dead 1990s Ada-compiler company (acquired by Rational, 1994) will pollute SEO for years. Small live collisions: a Telegram AI project and a Norwegian consultancy. verdix.ai serves a page — inspect before filing.

**Domain form:** `verdix.ai` (acquire) or `verdix.io`.

## 3. Accession (4 pts)
**Identity:** Every SEC filing has an accession number — the brand name and the clickable provenance chip are literally the same object.

**Honest risks:** Common dictionary word — weak trademark exclusivity. Nine letters, "ascension" confusion, and the meaning needs the UI to teach it. accession.ai (registered 2022) serves a page.

**Domain form:** `accession.app` or `getaccession.com`.

---

## Recommendation
**Winner: Assay.** Runner-up: **Verdix** — hold it as the fallback if assay.ai/getassay diligence surfaces a live conflict.

**Tagline:** *Assay — every claim tested, stamped, and on the record.* AI research you can grade, because we already graded it ourselves.
